Paint Correction

Paint correction is the art of taking out the swirls and surface-level scratches in your car or truck. Our high-end ceramic product includes some paint correction but we also offer it separately. It is important to understand the difference between polishing and paint correction. When a vehicle is detailed, we polish it. This means we use a compound and buffer to bring back the shine. When we paint correct a vehicle we make additional passes to remove more of the spidering and scratches. We offer single-step paint correction and multi-step depending on your needs and budget.
